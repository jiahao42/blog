About Ground Oddity

Ground Oddity is the personal blog of someone, and is named after the famous song of David Bowie, Space Oddity.

The purpose of this website is to share my thoughts typically in the field of programming, travelling, and life, etc.

The source code of this website is public, you may find it here. You're welcome to open issues in the repo about your thoughts/comments regarding this website.

Oh, and, just in case you want to follow me on Twitter :)

Enjoy :)
